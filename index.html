Pioneering Sustainable Packaging Since 2009

GreenWrap was founded on a simple belief: packaging should never cost the earth more than it's worth. We partner with food businesses, restaurants, and retailers to replace single-use plastic with beautiful, functional, and fully compostable alternatives.

Every product we make is certified food-safe, manufactured from responsibly sourced materials, and designed to break down — not pile up. From the Himalayan foothills to urban kitchens, our packaging travels India without leaving a scar.
